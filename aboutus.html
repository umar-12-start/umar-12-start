ABOUT US

Obtuse Tec Engineering and construction limited (Otec)

was registered company in Nigeria which was formed in the year 2013,

and engages in providing general engineering services,

general investment, supplies and installation,

general building and construction of houses,

express roads and consultancy services.

Otec Company was located in Maiduguri, Borno state.

  We provide complete construction solutions. The majority of our contracts are

'design & build' projects. We are involved in each stage of the project,

from architectural design, planning approval and construction.

We also provide building maintenance and facility management solutions.

This holistic approach delivers cost effectiveness,

streamlines project management and shortens construction timelines.
